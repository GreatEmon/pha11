Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Pioneering new ideas and solutions to drive progress and create value for the future

Sustainability

Commitment to protecting the environment and ensuring resources are preserved for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Building and enhancing systems that support growth, connectivity, and resilience.

Empowerment

Fostering opportunities and enabling individuals and communities to achieve their full potential.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Poverty is not an inherent condition; it is a failure of the systems we have built. Every human being has the potential to rise beyond their circumstances, but they need opportunities, not handouts. By fostering social businesses, empowering individuals, and innovating systems that prioritize human dignity, we can eradicate poverty, ensure sustainability, and build a world where every person can thrive with purpose and independence. The future we want is within our grasp if we work together to create it."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
